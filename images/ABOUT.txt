Porirua Pasifika Community Patrol is set up to support a safe and resilient community for all to thrive in.

We are a group of volunteers who work together to support a safe and resilient community for all to thrive in.


We patrol the streets, report incidents assists the police and other agencies and participate in various community projects.

Through being a member of a community patrol, people can create relationships throughout their local community and learn how to protect one another through working with police. Are you interested to be part of this and make our community safe, contact us.

 
https://givealittle.co.nz/org/porirua-pasifika-community-patrol-charitable-trust?fbclid=IwAR1nO6LmwTJsZQNO2lT3sAk724DBhhYlkMiRdHTFJpKSkVuug3Ws1pnS7lI

We are volunteers who want to keep the Porirua a safe place to live and be with whanau. The Patrol provides Community Patrol New Zealand's training to all patrollers, as well as on the job support.

Our patrol car has been a trusty and sometimes temperamental vehicle.

We rely on donations to train our volunteers at the annual Training Day in useful skills such as First Aid, maintenance and fuel for the Patrol car and for PPE.

You may have seen us if you live and work in Whitby, Aotea, Titahi Bay, Porirua CBD, Kenepuru, Cannons Creek and surrounding areas.

For the 2022/2023 year we travelled 2838km, volunteered 281 hours and reported 38 vehicle related, 12 property related and 16 people related to the Police for further steps, to name just a few.

Our patrollers' presence makes a difference every evening we are out and about, as an extension of the Police's "eyes and ears".
